Carrie Fisher's daughter, actress Billie Lourd, honored her mom with a song on the two-year anniversary of her death. "It has been two years since my Momby's death and I still don't know what the 'right' thing to do on a death anniversary is (I'm sure a lot of you feel the same way about your loved ones). So I decided to do something a little vulnerable for me, but something we both loved to do together - sing," Lourd wrote in an emotional Instagram post, which was accompanied by a video.  Fisher died on December 27, 2016 of undetermined causes after suffering a cardiac event on a flight from London to Los Angeles. She was 60.Fisher's mother and Lourd's grandmother, actress Debbie Reynolds, died one day later.
